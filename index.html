About Me

I've always seen myself as equally left- and right-brained, but it wasn’t until after college that I discovered the perfect way for me to use both — user experience design.

I graduated from Oakland University in 2018 with a bachelor’s in graphic design. My favorite thing about graphic design was figuring out the best way to bring text, imagery, and graphics together on a page. Now I enjoy doing the same thing for digital products! I also enjoy conducting user research and running usability tests to improve and validate my designs.

When I’m not designing, I’m either ballroom dancing or traveling and trying new foods. Hit me up if you ever need designer, or a dance teacher!
